About the project

This platform presents a comprehensive map of the relationships between 3,001 blood and urinary biomarkers and 21 neuropsychiatric disorders.

Genome-wide summary statistics for 28 blood biochemistry, 29 blood count, 4 urine biochemistry, and 2,940 blood proteomic biomarkers, as well as 11 mental disorders and 10 neurological disorders, were obtained from publicly available databases.

Key Features:

  • Genetic correlation analysis between biomarkers and disorders
  • Causal inference using Mendelian randomization
  • Interactive data visualization and filtering
  • Comprehensive statistical analysis results
